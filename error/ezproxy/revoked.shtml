Privileges Revoked

Your access to the proxy server and the libraries resources have been temporarily revoked, likely because of excessive or inappropriate use of an internet resource subscribed to by the library. Usually, this is a result of downloading an excessive number of journal articles in a short period of time which is a violation of our user agreements.

You might also see this message if you are no longer officially affiliated with the University.

If you have questions or concerns, or would like to discuss why your privileges may have been revoked, please contact proxy support and we will explain the circumstances and how, if it is possible, you may regain your access privileges.
