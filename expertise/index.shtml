Navari Family Center for Digital Scholarship

Expertise

The Navari Family Center for Digital Scholarship offers cross-disciplinary expertise in data use, analysis, and visualization, geographic information systems (GIS), digital humanities, metadata and digitization, research data services, and text mining and analysis.

Navari Family CDS staff also provide support for 3D modeling and printing, virtual reality development, and multimedia content creation.

To meet specialty needs, the Navari Family CDS has developed partnerships with various campus research providers, such as the Center for Research Computing and ND Research.

Our structure ensures that areas of expertise will evolve to meet the changing demands of our University community for research and scholarship in the 21st century.

CENTER SERVICES

Consultations and instruction. Meet with us one-on-one, attend a workshop, or view past workshop slides. Suggest a workshop topic or request a custom session. Find out how we can help bring digital scholarship tools and methods to your class.

Technology. Use our dual-monitor computer lab, access our specialized software, borrow equipment, print 3D objects, or use our new Legacy Technology Collection.

Technology-enriched spaces. Teach in our Lecture-Capture Classroom, quickly connect to one of our large collaborative monitors, use our Transcription Station to more efficiently transcribe multimedia files, or use our Data Visualization Lab to conduct your data- or visually-intensive scholarship.
