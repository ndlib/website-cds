Navari Family Center for Digital Scholarship

Spaces

The Navari Family Center for Digital Scholarship is located on the NE corner of the 2nd floor of the Hesburgh Library. The floor plan intentionally combines a number of different functional spaces, which are intended to encourage interaction, collaboration, and support advanced inquiry.
