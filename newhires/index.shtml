Welcome

We are excited for you to join us!

Please complete the "CDS Setup Form" linked above to enable us to begin the procurement process for relevant items as soon as you are able. (The more time we have to process purchases, the better!)

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact cds@nd.edu.


Timeline

  • Prior to arrival
    • Please complete the CDS Setup Form
    • You will receive the following information from your supervisor:
      • Date/time of arrival
      • Meeting location
      • Parking information (Bulla Lot, East of the Library and Library Lot)
      • Dress code information (as applicable)

  • Your first week [more information and step-by-step instructions]
    • Within 3 business days of the start date of your employment, you must complete your employment eligibility verification (I-9)
    • After your submit your I-9 paperwork to HR, your ND ID card and NDID number will be provided to you
    • Activate your NetID
    • After receiving your ND ID card and NDID number, to apply for a parking permit, take your ND ID card and license plate number to the Parking Services office in Hammes Mowbray Hall OR apply online (active NetID required) via insideND > iNDcars.
    • Complete payroll information via InsideND
    • Set up your ND email account
    • Confirm your directory information
    • Set up your telephone
    • Attend new hire Onboarding sessions (required)
