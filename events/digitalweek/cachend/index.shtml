GeocacheND: Race for the Cache

2014 Digital Week


Open to all students, faculty, and staff. Open to both individual or team participation.

Sunday, October 5
11 AM - 1 PM
Start at CDS to access the coordinates and fortify yourself with coffee and pastries!

How it Works

  • Download a geocaching app on your smartphone (or simply use Google Maps!), or,
    check out a GPS unit from the CDS when you arrive
  • Get a copy of the coordinates from the CDS Coordinator
  • Use the coordinates to seek out the locations of a series of hidden caches
  • Follow the instructions at the cache locations to log your find
  • Points will be given for each cache located
  • Prizes will be awarded for the most number of caches logged (in the event of a tie, a random number generator will be used to select prize winners)
