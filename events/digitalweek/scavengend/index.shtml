ScavengeND: Digital Scavenger Hunt

2014 Digital Week


Open to all students, faculty, and staff.

Winners will be announced the week of 10/13!

How it works

  • Join the Sakai site (login to Sakai > My Workspace > Membership > Joinable Sites > Digital Scavenger Hunt > Join)
  • Answer as many questions as you can between 5 PM on 10/4 and 4 PM on 10/9.
  • New questions will appear randomly throughout the week, so check back daily!
  • Responses will be graded throughout the week, and at the end of the week, prizes will be awarded for the most points earned, and some prizes will be awarded at random!
    • Grand Prize: iPad Mini
    • Second Prize: $50 gift card to Rohr's at the Morris Inn
    • Third Prize: $20 gift card to any Food Services location
    • Additional random prizes: tote bags, travel mugs, gift vouchers to Au Bon Pain, and more!
