3D Printing Contest

2014 Digital Week


Open to all students, faculty, and staff. No experience necessary.

Winners will be announced the week of 10/13

Prizes will be awarded for overall best design, creativity, and the most "loved" design in the Thingiverse contest collection, on Thursday, October 9. Winners will be notified via email, and additional announcements will be made via Twitter (#NDDigitalWeek) and posted to the CDS and Office of Digital Learning web sites.

Design Assignment

Design or improve upon an existing design for a functional object or accessory used in everday life.

Submission Deadline

5 PM on Wednesday, October 8.

Rules

  • Designs must be created in the free, online application Tinkercad.
  • Maximum dimensions: X (l) 250 mm/9.8 in | Y (w) 160 mm/6.3 in | Z (h) 150 mm/5.9 in
  • Files must be uploaded to Thingiverse by the submission deadline (Wed., 10/8/14 at 5 PM). (The CDS will move all eligible, properly-tagged submissions to the contest collection page for voting.)
  • The Thingiverse "Thing Info" description must contain a brief explanation of your object/accessory's function, or, if it is an improvement on an existing object/accessory, why your design is an enhancement to the existing model.
  • Thingiverse files must contain tags #NDDigitalWeek and #CDS.
  • Entries must conform to the CDS 3D Printing Limitations.
